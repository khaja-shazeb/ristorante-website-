Our Story

More Than Just a Cup of Coffee

Born from a deep love for the craft, Brewed & Co. opened its doors in 2018 with one simple mission: to make every sip an experience worth savoring.

We source our beans directly from small-scale farmers across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — building relationships that span seasons and sustain communities.
